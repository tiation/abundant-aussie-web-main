Professional Services Disclaimer

Important Notice
The information and services provided by Caleb Stands Strong are not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment from qualified healthcare providers.

Nature of Services

Caleb Standing is a qualified Support Coordinator who provides:

  • NDIS Support Coordination: Assistance with navigating NDIS supports and services
  • Information and Resources: Educational content about mental health and wellbeing
  • Advocacy Support: Help with accessing appropriate services and supports
  • Connection Facilitation: Assistance with linking to appropriate professional services

What We Do NOT Provide

  • Medical diagnosis or treatment
  • Psychological therapy or counselling (unless specifically qualified)
  • Crisis intervention or emergency response
  • Legal advice or representation
  • Medication management or advice
  • 24/7 crisis support services

Medical and Mental Health Disclaimer

Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for:

  • Mental health concerns requiring clinical assessment
  • Medication-related questions or changes
  • Diagnosis of mental health conditions
  • Treatment planning for serious mental illness
  • Any situation requiring immediate medical attention

Crisis and Emergency Situations

If You Are in Crisis

Support coordination services are not appropriate for crisis situations. Please contact emergency services immediately if you or someone else is:

  • Threatening to harm themselves or others
  • Experiencing thoughts of suicide
  • In immediate physical danger
  • Experiencing severe mental health symptoms
  • Under the influence of substances and at risk

Do not wait - call 000 or go to your nearest emergency department.

Limitations and Scope

Professional Boundaries

As a Support Coordinator, Caleb Standing operates within defined professional boundaries:

  • Scope of Practice: Services are limited to support coordination as defined by NDIS guidelines
  • Referral Responsibility: We will refer you to appropriate professionals when needs exceed our scope
  • Collaborative Approach: We work alongside, not instead of, your existing healthcare team

Information Accuracy

While we strive to provide accurate and up-to-date information:

  • Content is for educational purposes only
  • Information may not be suitable for all individual circumstances
  • Professional advice should always be sought for specific situations
  • External links are provided for convenience but we don't control their content
