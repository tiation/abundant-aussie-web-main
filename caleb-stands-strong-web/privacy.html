Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Caleb Stands Strong, we understand that privacy is fundamental to building trust, especially in mental health and support services. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number when you contact us
  • Communication Content: Messages you send through our contact forms or email
  • Scheduling Information: Preferred times and dates for consultations
  • Support Needs: Information you choose to share about your support requirements

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referring websites
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address (anonymized)
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to improve site functionality

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To respond to your inquiries and provide support coordination services
  • Communication: To schedule appointments and send service-related updates
  • Improvement: To enhance our website and services based on usage patterns
  • Legal Compliance: To meet our obligations under Australian privacy laws

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to information sharing
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us deliver services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety
  • Emergency Situations: To prevent serious harm to you or others

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Access Controls: Strict limits on who can access personal information
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and improvements
  • Secure Storage: Information stored on secure, Australian-based servers

Your Rights Under Australian Privacy Law

Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint about privacy practices
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications at any time

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve website functionality and user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to external resources and support services. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
